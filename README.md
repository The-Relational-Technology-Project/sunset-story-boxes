# Sunset Story Boxes

A community storytelling platform for the Outer Sunset, modeled on the idea of a "little free library" — but for neighborhood stories told live at local venues.

## What is this?

Sunset Story Boxes invites Outer Sunset residents to share, discover, and gather around hyper-local stories rooted in real neighborhood places. Browse story teasers, express interest in hearing them told live, sign up for alerts when a telling gets scheduled, or submit your own story.

## Features

- **Story browsing** — Discover short teasers for neighborhood stories tied to real places
- **Interest signups** — Let storytellers know you want to hear their story live
- **Event alerts** — Get notified when a story telling gets scheduled at a local venue
- **Story submission** — Submit your own neighborhood stories to the collection

## Tech stack

- [React](https://react.dev/) + [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) + [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/)
